Who’s Free is a mobile application used to tell a person or people that you are available at this moment, also you can make events that can be show to specific person or group. The event will has title and date and time and location. If any person of that group who can see the event, has interest in the event he can join it. All events that made to be seen for the application user will be show as TimeLine.

it's hard to know and tell whether a person or a group of people are free at the current moment in efficient and easy way. and it’s also a waste of time telling and arranging future event with others. Today, people use the normal way to check availability and arrange events either by calling or texting and this will waste your time and money. “Who is Free” will be designed for this purpose giving you the ability to find available people at the current time and arrange an event with them.
